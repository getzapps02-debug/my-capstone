# Source Reconciliation - Product Brief

## Verdict

Strong reconciliation overall. Core scope and mechanics were preserved.

## Gaps Found and Resolved

1. **Graph restraint** — Added product behavior requiring graph evidence to appear only when it clarifies a finding, with plain-language explanation and no requirement to interpret a dense network.
2. **Trust-focused usability test** — Expanded SM-3 to require distinction between source facts and system inference, recognition of uncertainty or missing evidence, and mandatory revision after any severe misleading interpretation.
3. **Visible privacy assurance** — Added an in-product requirement to explain local data handling and product limitations.
4. **Portfolio communication** — Added SM-9 to verify that the repository and walkthrough communicate the problem, evidence model, privacy boundary, interpretation limits, and known limitations without installation.

## Correctly Preserved

- Guided Investigation
- One Account per Investigation
- Sample, manual, and CSV onboarding
- Historical Account Balance and Category charts
- Evidence-backed Contributor ranking
- Non-causal and non-advisory language
- Synchronized Transaction Replay
- User control over thresholds, periods, classifications, and corrections
- Local persistence with no external transmission
- Explicit non-goals and refusal under insufficient evidence
